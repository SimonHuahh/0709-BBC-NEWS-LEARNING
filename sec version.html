Before You Read

先建立背景，再进入原文。财经新闻最重要的是抓住：原因、数据、行业影响、投资者反应。

背景导入

这篇新闻讲的是：受全球 AI 内存芯片需求推动，三星预计利润大幅增长。文章进一步解释了半导体供不应求、芯片价格上涨、AI 数据中心需求、韩国与亚洲其他地区加大芯片投资等背景。

这类文章非常适合学习商业英语，因为它包含了利润、预测、供需、投资、股价、产业竞争等高频财经表达。

带着 4 个问题阅读

  • Why did Samsung’s profits rise so sharply?
  • What happened to demand and supply in the chip market?
  • How did investors react to Samsung’s forecast?
  • How are Asian countries and firms responding to chip demand?

财经新闻阅读公式

Event → Cause → Data → Market Reaction → Industry Trend

事件：三星利润大涨；原因：AI 芯片需求；数据：利润、销售额、股价；反应：投资者预期；趋势：亚洲加大芯片投资。

Article Reading｜原文分段精读

高亮规则：蓝色=财经数据，绿色=增长/需求，橙色=供需价格，紫色=公司/政府动作，红色=市场反应。

Paragraph 1 · Main Event

South Korean technology giant Samsung Electronics says it expects to post a 19-fold jump in its profits, driven by global demand for artificial intelligence (AI) memory chips.

一句话理解：三星预计利润增长 19 倍，主要由全球 AI 内存芯片需求推动。
讲解：A expects to post a jump in B, driven by C. 这是商业新闻里非常实用的“结果 + 原因”结构。
可迁移：The company expects to post a sharp rise in revenue, driven by strong demand for online services.
Paragraph 2 · Profit Forecast

The company forecast that it made 89.4tn won (£43.6bn; $58.4bn) between the start of April and the end of June, marking its third record quarterly operating profits in a row.

一句话理解：三星预计 4 月到 6 月取得 89.4 万亿韩元，并连续第三个季度创下季度经营利润纪录。
讲解：forecast 是财经新闻高频词，表示“预测/预估”。marking... 是补充结果，表示“这标志着……”。
可迁移：The firm forecast strong sales for the second quarter, marking its best performance in three years.
Paragraph 3 · Why Firms Release Forecasts

Major South Korean firms like Samsung release forecasts of their earnings ahead of official detailed reports to help guide investors.

一句话理解：三星这类韩国大公司会在正式详细财报前发布盈利预测，帮助投资者判断。
讲解：release forecasts of their earnings = 发布盈利预测；to help guide investors = 为了给投资者提供指引。
可迁移：Listed companies often release forecasts of their earnings to help guide investors.
Paragraph 4 · Demand vs Supply

Samsung's latest forecast, released on Tuesday ahead of its full results due later in July, comes as demand for semiconductors continues to outstrip supplies - which has pushed up prices.

一句话理解：三星在正式财报前发布预测，而此时半导体需求持续超过供应，从而推高价格。
讲解：Demand for X outstrips supply, which pushes up prices. 这是解释价格上涨的经典供需句型。
可迁移：Demand for housing continues to outstrip supply, which has pushed up rents in major cities.
Paragraph 5 · Sales Data

Samsung said in the preview, known as earnings guidance, that it brought in around 171tn won of sales during the quarter, more than double the amount for the same period last year.

一句话理解：三星在盈利指引中表示，本季度销售额约 171 万亿韩元，是去年同期两倍多。
讲解：earnings guidance = 盈利指引；brought in sales/revenue = 实现销售额/营收。
可迁移：The company brought in record revenue during the quarter, more than double the amount a year earlier.
Paragraph 6 · Analyst Comment

The company's projected earnings mark one of "the best quarterly performances ever", which was close to the tech sector record set by Nvidia earlier this year, said industry analyst Marc Einstein from Counterpoint Research.

一句话理解：分析师称，三星预计业绩是史上最佳季度表现之一，接近 Nvidia 今年早些时候创下的科技行业纪录。
讲解：projected earnings = 预计盈利；one of the best... ever = 史上最佳之一；record set by... = 由……创下的纪录。
可迁移：The company’s projected earnings mark one of its strongest performances ever.
Paragraph 7 · AI Boom

"This has everything to do with the AI boom as memory companies continue to ride a tidal wave driven by limited supply and unprecedented demand," he added.

一句话理解：分析师认为，这完全与 AI 热潮有关，内存公司正在受益于供应有限和前所未有的需求。
讲解：ride a tidal wave 是形象表达，表示“乘上巨大浪潮”。unprecedented demand = 前所未有的需求。
可迁移：Streaming platforms rode a tidal wave of demand during the pandemic.
Paragraph 8 · Price Hikes

Samsung has hiked the prices of its memory chips as supplies remain tight.

一句话理解：由于供应仍然紧张，三星已经上调了内存芯片价格。
讲解：hike prices = 大幅提高价格；supplies remain tight = 供应仍然紧张。
可迁移：Food supplies remained tight after extreme weather damaged crops.
Paragraph 9 · Industry Context

Research firm IDC said demand for semiconductors for data centres and other AI infrastructure has been "different from anything the memory industry has navigated", impacting the supply of chips for everyday electronics.

一句话理解：IDC 表示，数据中心和 AI 基础设施对半导体的需求不同以往，并影响到日常电子产品芯片供应。
讲解：data centres and AI infrastructure 是 AI 产业链的重要表达；impacting... 是现在分词补充结果。
可迁移：Demand for AI infrastructure is reshaping the global semiconductor market.
Paragraph 10 · Tight Supply Continues

"We do expect supplies to be tight through next year given the unabated demand from AI data centres," said tech devices researcher Bryan Ma from IDC.

一句话理解：IDC 研究员预计，由于 AI 数据中心需求持续不减，供应紧张会持续到明年。
讲解：given = considering / because of，表示“鉴于；由于”。unabated demand = 未减弱的需求。
可迁移：Given the strong demand for cloud services, chip supplies may remain tight.
Paragraph 11 · Market Boom

Samsung is one of the world's biggest semiconductor manufacturers, making chips for firms like Nvidia and Google along with its range of electronic devices. The shares of major tech firms have soared in recent months due to surging demand for chips.

一句话理解：三星是全球最大半导体制造商之一，为 Nvidia 和 Google 等公司生产芯片；主要科技公司股价因芯片需求激增而上涨。
讲解：soar / surge 都表示快速上涨，但 soar 更常用于价格、股价、需求等明显上升。
可迁移：Shares of renewable energy firms have soared due to surging demand for clean power.
Paragraph 12 · Investor Reaction

Samsung's shares fell by almost 7% in Seoul on Tuesday as some investors had expected its profits to have been even higher.

一句话理解：尽管利润大涨，但由于部分投资者预期更高，三星股价周二在首尔下跌近 7%。
讲解：这是财经新闻里很典型的“好消息反而股价跌”：因为市场已经提前计入了更高预期。
可迁移：The company’s shares fell despite strong earnings because investors had expected even better results.
Paragraph 13 · Stock Value

Its stock market value has more than doubled since the start of this year, while South Korean rival SK Hynix has jumped by more than 200%.

一句话理解：三星市值今年以来已翻倍多，而韩国竞争对手 SK Hynix 上涨超过 200%。
讲解：more than doubled = 翻了一倍多；jumped by more than 200% = 上涨超过 200%。
可迁移：The value of the company has more than doubled since it entered the AI market.
Paragraph 14 · National Index

The strong performance of both companies has helped lift the value of South Korea's benchmark share index, the Kospi, by more than 80% this year.

一句话理解：两家公司强劲表现帮助韩国基准股指 Kospi 今年上涨超过 80%。
讲解：lift the value of... = 推高……价值；benchmark share index = 基准股指。
可迁移：Strong performance in the tech sector helped lift the country’s benchmark index.
Paragraph 15 · Nvidia Comparison

In May, Nvidia posted record quarterly sales and profits, with revenue between January and March topping $80bn.

一句话理解：Nvidia 在 5 月公布创纪录季度销售额和利润，1 月到 3 月营收超过 800 亿美元。
讲解：post record quarterly sales and profits = 公布创纪录季度销售额和利润；revenue topping... = 营收超过……。
可迁移：The company posted record quarterly revenue, with sales topping $10bn.
Paragraph 16 · Competition Worry

But Nvidia's stock fell at the time, in what some analysts said was a sign that investors were increasingly worried about rising competition in the sector.

一句话理解：但 Nvidia 当时股价也下跌，一些分析师认为这说明投资者越来越担心行业竞争加剧。
讲解：in what some analysts said was a sign that... = 一些分析师认为这表明……。这是新闻中引用观点的常见结构。
可迁移：The decline was seen as a sign that investors were worried about slowing demand.
Paragraph 17 · Investment Plans

In June, South Korea unveiled plans for at least $880bn of investments in projects led by Samsung and SK Hynix to build out the country's chip manufacturing in the coming years.

一句话理解：韩国 6 月公布至少 8800 亿美元投资计划，由三星和 SK Hynix 主导，用于未来几年扩建芯片制造能力。
讲解：unveil plans = 公布计划；build out = 扩建、完善、大规模建设。
可迁移：The government unveiled plans to build out the country’s renewable energy infrastructure.
Paragraph 18 · Regional Competition

Rival Asian firms in Japan, China and Taiwan are also investing heavily in chip factories to meet soaring demand.

一句话理解：日本、中国和台湾的亚洲竞争企业也在大量投资芯片工厂，以满足快速增长的需求。
讲解：invest heavily in... to meet soaring demand 是非常实用的商业表达，意思是“为满足激增需求而大量投资于……”。
可迁移：Many countries are investing heavily in renewable energy to meet soaring electricity demand.

Expression Lab｜重点表达实验室

按“词汇、短语、句型”三类整理，方便直接迁移到雅思/托福、商业英语和新闻复述里。

soar

to rise very quickly

中文飙升；猛增；快速上涨

原文AI chip sales soar = AI 芯片销量大幅上涨。

同义surge, jump

Demand for electric vehicles has soared in many countries.

forecast

to predict something in the future

中文预测；预估；预报

原文Samsung forecast that it made 89.4tn won.

同义predict, estimate

Analysts forecast that the global economy will grow more slowly next year.

outstrip

to become greater than something else

中文超过；超出；常用于供不应求

原文demand for semiconductors continues to outstrip supplies.

同义exceed, surpass

In many cities, demand for housing has outstripped supply.

unprecedented

never having happened before

中文前所未有的；史无前例的

原文unprecedented demand = 前所未有的需求。

同义exceptional, extraordinary

The company experienced unprecedented growth after launching its new product.

unveil

to officially announce or show something new

中文公布；推出；揭晓

原文South Korea unveiled plans = 韩国公布计划。

同义announce, reveal

The government unveiled a new policy to support renewable energy.

post a jump in profits

中文公布利润增长；实现利润上涨

Samsung expects to post a 19-fold jump in its profits.

同义report a rise in profits, record a profit increase

The airline posted a sharp jump in profits after travel demand recovered.

driven by global demand

中文由全球需求推动

三星利润大涨是 driven by global demand for AI memory chips。

同义fueled by global demand, boosted by global demand

The growth of the industry was driven by global demand for clean energy.

ahead of official detailed reports

中文在正式详细报告发布之前

韩国大公司会在正式财报前先发布盈利预测。

同义before official reports, prior to full reports

The company released its sales forecast ahead of official detailed reports.

supplies remain tight

中文供应仍然紧张

Samsung has hiked prices because supplies remain tight.

同义remain limited, stay constrained

Food supplies remained tight after the extreme weather damaged crops.

build out

中文扩建；完善；大规模建设

build out the country’s chip manufacturing = 扩大并完善本国芯片制造能力。

同义expand, develop

The company plans to build out its cloud infrastructure over the next five years.

原文Major firms release forecasts of their earnings ahead of official detailed reports to help guide investors.

原文Demand for semiconductors continues to outstrip supplies, which has pushed up prices.

亮点适合雅思 Task 1 / Task 2 解释价格上涨。

原文Memory companies continue to ride a tidal wave driven by limited supply and unprecedented demand.

原文Rival Asian firms are also investing heavily in chip factories to meet soaring demand.

Practice Zone｜互动练习区

通过填空、改写、总结，把输入变成输出。

1. 词汇选择

Demand for semiconductors continues to ______ supplies.

正确答案：B. outstrip。意思是“超过；超出”，常用于 demand outstrips supply。

2. 短语替换

把句子改得更正式：Samsung made much more profit because people around the world wanted AI chips.

Samsung posted a sharp jump in profits, driven by global demand for AI chips.

3. 句型仿写

请用句型：Demand for X outstrips supply, which pushes up prices.

中文提示：住房需求超过供应，从而推高了租金。

Demand for housing outstrips supply, which has pushed up rents.

4. 80词英文总结

用 60–80 词总结本文。建议使用：forecast, profits, AI memory chips, outstrip supplies, tight, invest heavily。

Samsung Electronics expects to post a 19-fold jump in profits, driven by global demand for AI memory chips. Demand for semiconductors continues to outstrip supply, pushing up prices and benefiting memory chip makers. Analysts say the AI boom has created unprecedented demand, especially from data centres and AI infrastructure. Although Samsung’s shares fell as some investors expected even higher profits, chip firms across Asia are investing heavily to meet soaring demand.

口语 Part 3 话题

How has artificial intelligence affected the global economy?

Artificial intelligence has created strong demand for advanced chips, data centres and cloud infrastructure. As a result, semiconductor companies have benefited from rising prices and unprecedented demand. However, this also creates pressure on supply chains and increases competition among countries.

写作 Task 2 话题

Some people believe governments should invest heavily in advanced technologies such as AI and semiconductors. To what extent do you agree?

可用表达：invest heavily in, build out infrastructure, meet soaring demand, drive economic growth, strengthen supply chains, rising competition, limited supply。
